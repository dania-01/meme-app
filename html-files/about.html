About MemeHub

Where Memes Meet Innovation 🚀

📅 Founded

MemeHub was born in 2024 when three friends decided that the world needed a place to turn memes into an experience — not just a laugh.

🎯 Our Mission

To empower global creators with a high-energy, interactive space where memes aren’t just shared — they’re celebrated, rated, and remembered.

🚀 Features

  • 🧠 AI Caption Generator for smart memes
  • 🏆 Leaderboard & Creator Badges for top memers
  • 📈 Real-time Meme Analytics
  • 🎨 Meme Studio with drag & drop tools
  • 💬 Comments, Voting, and Fun Mode
